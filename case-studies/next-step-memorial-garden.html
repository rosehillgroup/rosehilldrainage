Next Step Trust Memorial Garden

First installation combining Rosehill Drainage Mats with Rosehill TPV® proves ideal for inclusive outdoor spaces

Case Studies › Next Step Memorial Garden

Project Overview

The Next Step Trust, a specialist care provider for adults with learning disabilities, complex health needs, and Autism, transformed an overgrown, disused area into a welcoming memorial garden designed for year-round use. With waterlogging and uneven terrain making the space inaccessible, the project became the first to combine Rosehill Drainage Mats with Rosehill TPV® — creating a smooth, free-draining, and wheelchair-accessible surface tailored to the needs of its users.

The Challenge

  • Previously unusable space: overgrown, muddy, and waterlogged after rain
  • Poor drainage: standing water and puddles made the area inaccessible
  • Uneven surfaces: unsuitable for wheelchair users and individuals with mobility aids
  • Requirement for a sensory-friendly, low-maintenance finish

The Solution

  • Rosehill Drainage Mats installed along the length of the garden to manage water below the surface
  • SBR rubber base layer laid over and around the mats to stabilise the foundation
  • Rosehill TPV® softfall surfacing applied on top, delivering a vibrant, cushioned, and seamless finish
  • Accessible pathway and play zone created in a single integrated surface
  • Water directed away from the active area to maintain performance in wet weather

Results

  • Fully accessible for wheelchair users and individuals with limited mobility
  • No standing water or pooling after rainfall — the surface remains usable in all seasons
  • Enhanced sensory experience with a safe, soft, and brightly coloured TPV finish
  • Durable and low-maintenance, ideal for long-term use in a community care setting
  • Proof of concept: this successful integration of Rosehill Drainage Mats and Rosehill TPV® sets the benchmark for similar inclusive installations

Why It Matters

This project showcases how intelligent drainage design and specialist surfacing can unlock the potential of otherwise inaccessible spaces. The upgraded memorial garden provides a therapeutic outdoor environment for the people supported by The Next Step Trust — encouraging relaxation, engagement with nature, and moments of quiet reflection in a safe, inclusive setting.
