Drainage Solutions

Modular, sustainable drainage systems combining outstanding hydraulic performance with environmental responsibility

Trough & Stacker System

Two-component modular system: Trough units channel water along the ditch floor while Stacker units placed on top filter surface water down into the trough below. Water then flows through the trough to connected catch pits. Perfect for trackside, highway, and infrastructure applications.

  • 176.4 m/hr vertical hydraulic conductivity
  • Fatigue tested to 506,000+ load cycles
  • 60+ years design life in standard conditions
  • Modular stackable design for custom capacity
Learn More

Drainage Mats

High-capacity surface and sub-surface drainage solution combining rapid installation with long-term durability in a lightweight format. Ideal for verges, embankments, and distributed drainage applications.

  • 49 litres/metre-sq/sec water permeability
  • Multiple thickness options (50-100mm)
  • Chemical and freeze-thaw resistant
  • Perfect for verges and embankments
