Our Product Range

Modular, sustainable drainage systems combining outstanding hydraulic performance with environmental responsibility. Each solution is engineered from recycled materials for demanding infrastructure applications.

Trough & Stacker System

Two-component modular system for trackside, highway, and infrastructure drainage. Trough units channel water along the ditch floor while Stacker units filter surface water down into the trough below.

176.4 m/hr Hydraulic conductivity
506k+ Fatigue test cycles
60+ yrs Design life
93% Less CO₂ vs concrete

Drainage Mats

High-capacity surface and sub-surface drainage combining rapid installation with long-term durability. Ideal for verges, embankments, sports fields, and distributed drainage applications.
