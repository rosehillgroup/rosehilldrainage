Our Story

Over three decades of polymer innovation and infrastructure expertise

From Polymer Innovation to Drainage Excellence

Founded in 1988, Rosehill established itself as a pioneer in polymer-based infrastructure solutions, building expertise in transforming recycled materials into high-performance products. Our decades of experience in sustainable manufacturing and engineering excellence laid the foundation for our recent expansion into advanced drainage systems.

Recognising the critical need for sustainable drainage solutions across transport infrastructure, we've applied our proven polymer expertise to revolutionise how drainage challenges are addressed. Today, we combine over 30 years of materials innovation with cutting-edge drainage technology to deliver solutions that protect both infrastructure and environment.

Our Sowerby Bridge Facility

Operating from our UK facilities in Sowerby Bridge, West Yorkshire, we maintain full production control over our entire manufacturing process. Our advanced cold-cure manufacturing process requires minimal energy while delivering exceptional performance.

  • 100% recycled rubber content in many products
  • Cold-cure process with minimal energy requirements
  • ISO 9001:2015 quality assurance system
  • Manufacturing control ensuring exceptional quality
  • UKAS accredited testing validation
  • National Highways and Network Rail approved
